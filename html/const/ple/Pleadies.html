Pleiades-Seven Sisters-M45

Pleiades is a group a seven stars (the seven sisters). That is in the constellation Taurus. The are made of hot blue and extremely luminous stars. Computer simulations have shown that the Pleiades was probably made from the Orion Nebula. Astronomers estimate that the cluster will survive for about another 250 million years (if there is 250,000,000 Years). Pleiades comes from Ancient Greeks. The nine brightest stars of the Pleiades are named for the Seven Sisters of Greeks: Sterope, Merope, Electra, Maia, Taygeta, Celaeno, and Alcyone, along with their parents Atlas and Pleione.
